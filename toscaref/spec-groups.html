Groups

groups provide a way of configuring shared behavior for different sets of node_templates. Groups can optionally have a type and properties.

As an Unfurl extension, groups can have other groups as members.

Example

groups:
  group1:
    type: my_group_type
    members:
      - node_template_1
      - another_group
  another_group:
    members:
      - node_template_2

no
